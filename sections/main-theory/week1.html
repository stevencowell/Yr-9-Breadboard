Understanding WHS Signage

Workplace Health and Safety (WHS) signage is critical in a workshop. These signs use specific colours and shapes to alert you to hazards:

  • Red: Indicates danger or prohibition (e.g. no entry zones).
  • Yellow: Warns of potential hazards such as slippery surfaces.
  • Blue: Provides information and instructions.
  • Green: Denotes the location of safety equipment and emergency exits.
