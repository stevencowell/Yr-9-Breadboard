Week 8: Timber Finishing and Sustainability

Introduction

Week 8 focuses on finishing your timber project and integrating sustainable practices. This article explores the different types of finishes available and discusses their environmental impact.

Types of Finishes

From clear varnishes to oil-based finishes like Danish Oil, each finish offers unique benefits. Understanding these differences helps you choose the right finish for both durability and aesthetics.

Application Techniques

Learn the step-by-step process for applying an even, long-lasting finish. Proper technique ensures that the timber is protected and its natural beauty is enhanced.

Sustainability in Timber Technology

Adopting sustainable practices is vital. This section covers the use of renewable resources, recycling, and conservation methods to minimise the environmental impact of timber production.
