Week 4: Decorative Techniques and Machinery

Introduction

Week 4 explores the creative side of timber technology. This article covers decorative techniques using modern machinery such as laser cutters and jigsaws to enhance both functionality and aesthetics.

Laser Cutting and Engraving

Laser cutters allow for precise engraving and cutting of intricate patterns. You will learn how computer-aided design (CAD) software is used to create custom patterns that can be applied to timber, opening up new possibilities for design.

Using the Jigsaw

The jigsaw is a versatile tool that can be used to create curves and complex shapes. Proper use of the jigsaw, combined with safety measures, is essential for achieving smooth and accurate cuts.

Design Considerations and Safety

While creativity is important, always prioritise safety. Planning your design, selecting appropriate tools, and following safety guidelines are critical for secure operation.
