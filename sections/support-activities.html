Week 1: Workshop Safety and Marking Tools

Working safely

In the workshop, follow safety rules: wear safety glasses, tie long hair, keep floors clean, and walk, not run. Always listen to instructions.

Marking and measuring

Pick a 'face side' and 'face edge' on your timber so all measurements start from the same place. Use rulers and try squares to draw straight lines and measure twice before cutting.
